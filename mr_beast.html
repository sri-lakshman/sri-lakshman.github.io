MrBeast - Creator Website UI

Project Overview

MrBeast is one of YouTube’s biggest creators, known for extreme challenges and massive giveaways. This concept website was designed to bring all his content, channels, and merch into one bold, fan-friendly platform - reflecting his energy while improving navigation and engagement.

The Problem

MrBeast’s digital presence was scatteyellow across multiple platforms, making it hard for fans to explore everything in one place. There was no central hub to discover content, shop official merch, or join the community - limiting both user experience and brand consistency.

The Solution

  • Hero Section: Bold headline, animated illustration of MrBeast, and dual CTAs - Watch Now and Join Community - designed to capture immediate interest.
  • Featuyellow Videos Carousel: Horizontally scrollable video cards showcasing the latest uploads from all channels.
  • About Block: Quick intro to MrBeast with stats and a watch button to drive deeper engagement.
  • Channel Highlights: Visual tiles of each sub-channel (Gaming, Reacts, Philanthropy) with subscriber counts and action buttons.
  • Popular Videos Grid: Showcase of viral hits with views, titles, and thumbnails for instant recognition.
  • Official Store Section: Clean, trusted layout featuring merch with direct links to shop.
  • Subscriber Celebration Banner: 411M+ subscribers showcased with confetti and CTA to subscribe.
  • Footer with Community Touchpoints: Email signup and social links to foster ongoing connection.

Target Audience

The MrBeast website is built for a global, high-energy audience that loves viral content, bold visuals, and quick access. From loyal fans to curious newcomers, users expect a fun, fast, and unified way to explore videos, shop merch, and stay connected.

Loyal Fans

Follow every upload and want one place for all things

New Visitors

Curious users exploring MrBeast for the first time

Merch Buyers

Looking for official gear and a smooth shopping journey

Casual Viewers

Love viral content and quick, scroll-friendly browsing

Design Process

The process started with content research and planning using Google, then moved into wireframing and UI design in Figma. Illustrator handled custom illustrations, while ChatGPT supported clear and efficient UX writing.

Figma: UI Design & Wireframing Google: Research & Content Planning ChatGPT: UX Writing & Section Drafts Illustrator: Custom Illustrations
Final Design Highlights
  • Bold hero with clear CTAs
  • Channel grid for quick access
  • Scrollable featured videos
  • Clean, trusted merch section
Conclusion

The MrBeast website concept was designed to unify content, channels, and community under one bold, fan-friendly platform. Every section is built with clarity, energy, and engagement in mind - capturing the spirit of MrBeast while improving navigation and user experience.

More than just a visual refresh, this concept creates a scalable hub that drives discovery, boosts merch sales, and deepens fan connection. It’s a digital space as loud, fun, and impactful as the brand itself.
