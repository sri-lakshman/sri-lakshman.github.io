Diginova – Creative Agency Website

Project Overview

Diginova is a future-forward creative agency that empowers businesses through tailored digital solutions — from branding to marketing. This website redesign was a self-initiated effort to sharpen the agency’s visual identity and improve engagement through intuitive design and storytelling.

The Problem

Diginova lacked an online presence that showcased its full creative capabilities. Without a central platform, the agency found it difficult to communicate its services, team expertise, and value proposition. A brand that thrives on creativity needed a digital presence to match.

The Solution

  • Hero Section: Impactful headline with custom illustration and dual CTAs that speak directly to user intent.
  • Service Highlights: Modular cards for Branding, Web Design, App Development, and Digital Marketing.
  • Stats Block: Visual confidence boosters with experience, projects, and satisfaction rate.
  • Why Choose Us: Feature-rich value props paired with illustrations to reinforce brand strengths.
  • Blog Integration: Resource section to showcase industry knowledge and boost SEO.
  • FAQ Accordion: Interactive Q&A section for visitor clarity and conversion.
  • Team Section: Real faces, real names — humanizing the agency.

Design Process

The process began with strategic planning and content mapping in Notion, followed by low-fidelity wireframes and clean UI in Figma. Adobe Illustrator was used for bespoke illustrations, while ChatGPT supported UX writing with clarity and speed.

Figma: UI Design & Wireframing Notion: Research & Content Planning ChatGPT: UX Writing & Section Drafts Illustrator: Custom Illustrations
Final Design Highlights
  • Interactive hero with instant CTA
  • Clear feature cards with benefits-first language
  • Visual 3-step “How it works” section
  • Simple, transparent pricing
Conclusion

The Diginova website design was built from the ground up to reflect the agency’s creative vision while addressing real user needs. With a focus on clarity, structure, and personality, the site establishes a professional digital presence that builds trust and inspires action.

This concept is more than just aesthetics — it's a scalable foundation for growth, storytelling, and stronger client relationships. Designed with intention, the platform positions Diginova as a forward-thinking agency ready to lead in the digital space.
