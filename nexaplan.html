NexaPlan - Simplifying Teamwork

Project Overview

NexaPlan is a future-ready project management platform designed to empower teams with smarter workflows and streamlined collaboration. From task management to progress tracking, NexaPlan brings everything together in one place. This redesign was a self-initiated effort to refine the platform’s visual identity and enhance engagement through intuitive design and clear storytelling.

The Problem

NexaPlan lacked a digital presence that fully represented its robust features and team-focused value. Without a cohesive platform identity, it was challenging to communicate its benefits, usability, and impact. A product built to simplify project management needed an online presence that reflected its innovation and reliability.

The Solution

  • Hero Section: Impactful headline, custom visuals, and clear CTAs to showcase NexaPlan’s value instantly.
  • Users Logos: Trusted by leading global brands, building instant credibility and trust.
  • About NexaPlan: Future-ready platform for task tracking, workflow automation, and seamless collaboration.
  • Pricing: Simple, transparent plans designed to scale with teams of any size.
  • FAQ: Quick answers to common questions for clarity and confidence.
  • CTA: Motivating call-to-action encouraging teams to get started with NexaPlan.

Target Audience

NexaPlan is designed for professionals and teams who value clarity, efficiency, and streamlined collaboration.

Freelancers & Small Teams

Manage clients, track tasks, and stay organized with ease

Project Managers

Plan projects, monitor progress, and align teams effectively

Growing Businesses

Simplify workflows and improve collaboration across teams

Enterprises

Ensure consistency, security, and efficiency at scale

Design Process

The NexaPlan design began with strategic planning and content mapping in Notion. Low-fidelity wireframes and polished UI were created in Figma, with custom illustrations crafted in Adobe Illustrator. Google Search and Google Gemini supported research and inspiration, while ChatGPT aided UX writing for clarity and speed.

Figma: Low-Fidelity Wireframes & Polished UI Google Search & Google Gemini: Research & Inspiration ChatGPT: UX Writing & Section Drafts Illustrator: Custom Illustrations
Final Design Highlights
  • Engaging interactive hero with immediate CTA
  • Feature cards emphasizing benefits-first messaging
  • Clear About and FAQ sections
  • Transparent Pricing layout
Conclusion

The NexaPlan website was designed from the ground up to capture the agency’s creative vision while meeting real user needs. Prioritizing clarity, structure, and personality, the site creates a professional digital presence that builds trust and drives action.

More than just visual appeal, this concept provides a scalable foundation for growth, storytelling, and stronger client connections. Thoughtfully designed, the platform positions NexaPlan as a forward-thinking agency ready to lead in the digital space.
