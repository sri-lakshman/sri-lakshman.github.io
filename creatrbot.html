CreatrBot - AI Content Assistant for Designers

Problem Statement

Many designers struggle with writing portfolio content, captions, or case studies-even though storytelling is key to showcasing their work. The existing AI tools are too broad, lack personality, or overwhelm with complexity. There’s a need for a content generation tool tailored for designers-one that speaks their language and fits into their creative workflow.

Objective

To design a clean, conversion-optimized landing page that:

  • Clearly communicates the value of CreatrBot in seconds
  • Provides an easy and playful way to try the product
  • Builds trust and encourages trial sign-ups
  • Explains key features in a visual, outcome-driven manner

Target Audience

These users value clarity, aesthetic simplicity, and fast results. They are visual-first thinkers who appreciate strong design with minimal cognitive load.

Freelance UI/UX Designers

For quick, clean case studies and project writeups

Brand & Visual Designers

To express their work in a clear, relatable voice

Portfolio Builders

To turn visuals into compelling stories-fast

Creative Directors

To maintain tone and consistency across teams

Research & Insights

Key Findings from Interviews, Forums & Feedback

  • “I know my process, but writing it out is a nightmare.”
  • “Tools like ChatGPT are great, but I need a tone that feels me.”
  • “Give me Instagram captions that don’t sound robotic.”

Competitive Analysis

Compared tools like Copy.ai, Jasper, and Writesonic. Found them too generic or business-centric. The opportunity lay in building a designer-first experience with niche tools and a personalized voice.

Design Process

Started with problem validation and IA. Built low-fidelity wireframes, followed by clean, readable UI components in Figma. Tailored UX writing using ChatGPT, Prepexility, and Lovable.ai.

Figma: UI Design Notion: Research, Planning ChatGPT: Drafting content sections Prepexility: Headline testing + emotional triggers Lovable.ai: Tone & relatability optimization
Final Design Highlights
  • Interactive hero with instant CTA
  • Clear feature cards with benefits-first language
  • Visual 3-step “How it works” section
  • Simple, transparent pricing
Outcomes (Projected)

Improved content clarity, reduced bounce, better engagement through simplified UX and designer-focused voice.

Conclusion

CreatrBot reimagines how designers create content - smarter, faster, and more personal. Built with care, clarity, and creativity in mind.
