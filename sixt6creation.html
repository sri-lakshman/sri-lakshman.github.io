Sixt6creation - Brand Guidelines

Project Overview

Sixt6creation is a creative studio that partners with startups and growing businesses to deliver bold brand identities and digital experiences. The goal of this project was to craft a strong, modern, and scalable visual system that reflects the studio’s creative energy and expertise across branding, web design, mobile UI, and marketing assets.

This identity was built to be more than just visually striking-it needed to be flexible, memorable, and consistent across both digital and print applications. From logo design and typography to color palette and brand assets, every element was thoughtfully crafted to express Sixt6creation’s values of clarity, creativity, and innovation.

User Research & Discovery

We focused on startups and creative professionals who needed bold, consistent branding that works across platforms. Research revealed common issues like weak recall and poor scalability-guiding us to build a flexible, high-impact brand system.

Strategy & Design Solutions

  • Logo System: A custom, scalable mark combining creativity and structure-ideal for both digital and print use.
  • Color Palette: Bold red with black and white for high contrast, energy, and visual impact.
  • Typography Hierarchy: Clean, geometric fonts that balance clarity and personality across platforms.
  • Visual Elements: Minimal layouts, abstract forms, and a signature red texture for strong brand recall.
  • Brand Applications: Tested on real-world assets-business cards, posters, UIs, and social media-for seamless consistency.
  • Guideline Assets: Includes clear rules for logo, color, type, and mockups to ensure easy implementation.

Design Process

The process began with visual research using Google, followed by logo and layout design in Figma. Illustrator was used for custom brand elements, while ChatGPT helped shape clear and consistent brand messaging.

Figma: Brand mockups, UI previews Illustrator: Logo and icon design Photoshop: Texture creation and visual assets Google: Trend research and inspiration ChatGPT: Content drafting, naming ideas, and creative feedback
Final Design Highlights
  • Bold logo with strong red accents
  • Clean, modern and minimal layout
  • Scalable assets for digital and print
  • Consistent and structured visual style
Conclusion

The Sixt6creation brand guideline brings structure to creativity-combining bold visuals with a clean, modern system. Every element was crafted to reflect the studio’s energy and forward-thinking approach.

Built for Consistency

This identity gives Sixt6creation a strong, unified voice across platforms. It’s a strategic foundation that drives trust, scalability, and confident brand presence.
