IppoPay - Conceptual Consumer UPI App

Problem Statement

IppoPay is strong with shop owners and small businesses, but for normal people, most still use GPay, PhonePe or Paytm. These apps feel heavy, crowded, and not always easy for first-time users in smaller towns. There is space for a simple UPI app that is local, friendly, and easy to use, while also connecting with IppoPay’s trusted merchant network.

Objective

To design a concept consumer app – IppoPay Now that:

  • Explains value in seconds, in a simple way
  • Lets users pay instantly with an easy flow
  • Builds trust with a local-first design
  • Shows key features like UPI payments, bill pay, offers, and rewards in a clear, visual way

Target Audience

Everyday UPI users who want payments to be quick, simple, and stress-free. They prefer clear design, less confusion, and instant results.

Students & Professionals

For easy transfers, splitting bills, and quick mobile recharges

Families & Homemakers

For smooth payments of electricity, gas, water bills and daily expenses

Small-Town Users

For simple UPI in local language with fast, friendly payments

Shoppers & Customers

For quick store payments, offers, and earning loyalty rewards

Research & Insights

Key Findings from Interviews, Forums & Feedback

  • “I use UPI daily, but these apps feel too crowded.”
  • “Sometimes I just want a simple way to pay bills fast.”
  • “I wish I could pay in my own language, not only English.”

Competitive Analysis

Apps like GPay, PhonePe, and Paytm are powerful but often too complex for new users, with more focus on big-city audiences than small towns. This created a chance to build a local-first, easy UPI app that simplifies payments and connects with everyday shops and rewards.

Design Process

Started by understanding users’ payment challenges, then created the information flow and low-fidelity wireframes. Designed clean, minimal UI screens in Figma and polished illustrations in Adobe Illustrator to make the app simple, local-friendly, and trustworthy.

Figma: For wireframes and UI design Adobe Illustrator: For illustrations, and visuals Google: For market and competitor insights ChatGPT: For drafting content and UX writing
Final Design Highlights
  • Instant Payments – Send, receive, split money
  • Local-Friendly – Works in Tamil, Hindi, English
  • Bill Payments – Mobile, DTH, electricity, gas
  • Shop Rewards – Pay nearby stores, earn points
Outcomes (Projected)

Simplified payments, faster transactions, better engagement, and increased trust through a clean, local-friendly UX.

Conclusion

IppoPay Now reimagines personal UPI payments – simple, instant, and connected to everyday shops. Built to be fast, friendly, and easy for everyone.
